For Rounds 1 - 10, place a st marker in the first st of each Rond. DO NOT begin with a Ch 1. Unless noted, DO NOT join with a slst at the end of each Round.

Round 1: with two strands of yarn held together, make the magic ring. Ch 1, work 8 hdc into the ring, DO NOT join, pull tail tightly to close ring (8).

Round 2: work 2 hdc in each st around (16)

Round 3: (hdc in next st, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (24)

Round 4: (hdc in each of the next 2 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (32)

At the end of Round 4, the piece should measure approximately 4.5" across.

Round 5: (hdc in each of the next 3 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (40)

Round 6: (hdc in each of the next 4 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (48)

Round 7: (hdc in each of the next 5 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (56)

Round 8: (hdc in each of the next 6 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (64)

Round 9: (hdc in each of the next 7 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around (72)

Round 10: (hdc in each of the next 8 sts, 2 hdc in next st) repeat around, slst in the first st to join (80)

At the end of Round 10, the bottom of the bag should measure approximately
11.5" across.  For Rounds 11 - 23 you will begin each Round with a Ch 1. The Ch
1 DOES NOT count as a st. At the end of each Round, sls in the first st to
join.

Round 11: Ch 1. (hdcblo in each of the next 3 sts, fpdc in next st) repeat around, join (80) Click directly on the highlighted link below to view the YouTube tutorial for Round 11. https://youtu.be/QR4QxEt6XFE

Round 12: Ch 1. hdcblo in each of the first 2 sts, fpdc in next st, (hdcblo in each of the next 3 sts, fpdc in next st) repeat around until 1 st remains, hdcblo in last st, join (80) Click directly on the highlighted link below to view the YouTube tutorial for Round 12. https://youtu.be/3eAyY_RiVgM

Round 13: Ch 1. hdcblo in the first st, fpdc in next st (hdcblo in each of the next 3 sts, fpdc in next st) repeat around until 2 sts remain, hdcblo in each of the last 2 sts, join (80) Click directly on the highlighted link below to view the YouTube tutorial for Round 13. https://youtu.be/bqMh7ZR9IOE

Round 14: Ch 1, fpdc in first st, (hdcblo in each of the next 3 sts, fpdc in next st) repeat around until 3 sts remain, hdcblo in each of he last 3 sts, join (80). Click directly on the highlighted link below to view the YouTube tutorial for Round 14. https://youtu.be/K5TmIS8tjr0
